Common Questions:
Does NBGS Travel require the travel payments all at once?

No, NBGS Travel does not require you to pay for your trip all at once. We offer flexible payment options to suit your financial planning:



Pay in Full: Settle the entire trip cost upfront.
Payment Plan: Opt for a structured installment plan, allowing you to pay over time.



To confirm your booking, we generally require a 25% deposit of the total package amount. However, certain packages may have a different deposit amount, which will be clearly stated at the bottom of the package details. Please review each trip's information for the exact deposit requirement.
